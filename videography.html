Video Background

Since earning a Bachelor's degree in Digital Media Technologies from ESU, I have amassed over 5 years of professional experience as a videographer and producer at Clarkson University. During my tenure, I have been the driving force behind the creation, shooting, and editing of a diverse range of content, including engaging tour videos, event recaps, and advertisements. My skill set encompasses the entire spectrum of video production, from conducting interviews and capturing b-roll footage to incorporating dynamic drone content. Embracing the multifaceted challenges inherent in video production, I take pride in my ability to deliver high-quality, visually captivating content that resonates with diverse audiences.
