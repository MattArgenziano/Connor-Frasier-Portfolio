Video Background

I've had a passion for filming my entire life. I love capturing a wide variety of motion images, and especially telling a visual story afterwards. I received my Bachelor's degree at ESU for Digital Media Technologies, and those 4 years gave me the tools I needed to start my career. As of 2022, I'm a Digital Content Creator/Writer at Clarkson University and enjoy every aspect of content creation and marketing. I'm very skilled in a wide variety of video styles, as well as drone videography.
